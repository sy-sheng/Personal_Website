Material Footprint

- Urban thing study: the material footprint during Milan Expo

  • [DATE]   09/2019 - 11/2019
  • [SITE]   Milan, Italy
  • [AREA]   0.459ha
  • [Personal Work]   Expo study, material footprint research

  • Expo chosen as my urban thing study since its a mega-event happening regularly. Each time it happens, an expo park built up and millions of people will come and visit. After the event,the expo part is abandoned as if nothing have happened. However, in the period, the expos are making great effect on a country's economy especially the area aroung the expo parks.

  • Thus, this project is trying to understand what was going on before, during and after the mega-event by trying to understand the material footprint through Expo and the social effects caused by it.
  • Before the Milan Expo, the site of expo park was an agricultural area. After the construction of the Milan Expo park, the area has become an area consist of buildings though most of them are temporary. The infrastructures are built for the expo, along with the water system.
  • However, after the expo, only few of them are left. The expo park has become an abandoned area with nothing.
  • To buid up the area, the material of the buildings are brought from all around the world. Some of the nations got the material from local supliers and some of others brought their materials from their own countries. The drawing shows the material consistence of the expo park.

  • Also, the producing of the materials, the transportation and the construction period are causing energy consumption and some environmental problems shown in the drawing and they are sorted by the influence shown by font size.
  • During the Expo, people gathering in the Expo park from all around the world causing different kinds of domestic garbages and also consuming materials such as food.

  • In Milan Expo, the Expo park has a inner circulating system to using the food waste to cultivating grain and friut supplying the need of Expo park. Also, the recicles are sent to specific trash centers for processing.
  • The recycles are transported to the garbage centers for classification and then some of them are sent to manufactories and made into products that can come back to the city such as clothes and plastic articles.

  • In this process of reusing, materials and energies area saved and emission have been redueced.
  • The material after deal with the garbage centers, the manufactories made them in to new products which make the mega-event more sustainable for the city.

  • In all this process, the transportation is always an important part. Through the process of the expo park building till the reusing of the waste, there are many types of transportation in the process. In this process, waste emission and energy consumption are also happening. The main transportation ways are listed in the drawing and the main problem caused by them has been labled.
