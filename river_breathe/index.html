Waterfront Breathe

- System design of Andalian River basin

  • [DATE]   11/2017 - 01/2018
  • [SITE]   Andalian River, Chile
  • [AREA]   347.26ha
  • [Personal Work]   Ecosystem design, resilient waterfront design, housing rebuilt

  • For people living in the Andalian River area, Chile, the river is an important consistance of their everyday life and life experience. However, although the river give the locals special experience of waterfront, the river also caused many problems such as flood and other urban disasters.

  • To improve the life experience of local people and reduce the damage caused by the river, this project is selecting three different typical site locates in different part of the river and trying to propose the ways to promote current living condition of the locals.
  • The project start with analysis of the area, the analysis is based on the land use functions and the hot spot of local activities. The three sites are chosen based on the relative location to the Andalian River.
  • Site I locates in river mouth where there are rarely human activies but a critical point to prevent urban disasters and improve soil quality. Site II locats in middle area where floods frequently happens and also the center area taking the resposibility of most commercial areas in the area. Site III locates in confluence area where mainly consist of small villages. The problem of flood brings the locals a lot of security problems.
  • SITE I: River Mouth Area

  • The river mouth suffered from several series soil problems. To change the situation, I am proposing the following possibilities of using local wetland plants to make the soil condition better and reduce the risk of flood on the bank. Using a more natural way to solve the nature problems is a new solution applied on the site.
  • SITE II: Middle & Commercial Area

  • The area locates in the middle area of the river. This area is mainly surrounded by commercial and residential areas. It is the "city center". Through the design, the river bank is more elastical and resistence to the disasters. At the same time, the open active spaces for locals are increased and people have sufficient spaces for more ample activities.
  • SITE III: Confluence Area

  • The residents of the site are often troubled by the floods. For this reason, I am proposing to change the surrounding lands into wetland which can reduce the amount of runoff on the surface where the flood occurred. The buildings area devided into different area based on the area they are suffering from.

  • After the flood area is decreased, not only the risk and damage caused by the flood is decreased, the landscape in the area is becoming more diverse and locals can have more activity areas.
  • SITE III: Confluence Area

  • While the flood areas are decreased, there are still some buildings are at risk. I am also proposing to ungrad the buildings so that when the flood occurred, they can keep safty. Also, in the days without floods, people can have more spcaes to do activities.

  • The second-floor spaces provide people with more living spaces while avoiding the threat of flooding. The corridor connects the buildings thus guarantee a certain range of activities even when the flood comes. Planting areas and activity areas are connected by corridors provide a richer activity for people.
